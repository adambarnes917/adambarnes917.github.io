Our Mission

We are a newly forming club dedicated to teaching the fundamentals of basketball, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Our Goals

  • Encourage teamwork and leadership

Founder

The Rising Stars Basketball Club was founded by Brian Saunders, who created the program to give young athletes a place to grow, learn, and develop a passion for basketball.
